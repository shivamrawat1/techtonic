About Us

Helping you ace both technical and behavioral interviews

Techtonic is a comprehensive platform designed to prepare you for all aspects of the interview process through AI-powered mock interviews and structured feedback.

Our platform helps you practice both technical problem-solving using the UMPIRE framework (Understand, Match, Plan, Implement, Review, Evaluate) and behavioral questions using the STAR method (Situation, Task, Action, Result).
